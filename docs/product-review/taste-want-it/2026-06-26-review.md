## Per-screen notes

### 1 — Discover
Correct but not magnetic. After tier-5 polish the cards scan fine, yet nothing says *why this game for me now*. I'd open Play to check, not because the feed pulled me.

### 2 — Decide
Too many equal-weight panels before I feel the decision. Join exists but competes with icon action bar, matching copy, reviews, regulars, tournaments. I wouldn't linger — I'd bounce.

### 3 — Commit
Joining doesn't feel like a commitment moment. No top status line ("You're in — Mon 7PM"). Coaching vertical (`ClassDetailScreen`) already nails this shape.

### 4 — Coordinate
Powerful for hosts, overwhelming for players. I don't *want* to open game room as a player — too much chrome.

### 5 — Close → reopen
Ends in operational attendance UI. No delight, no next-game hook. Loop dies here.
