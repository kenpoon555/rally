## What worked

- **Play · Games empty** (`DiscoverEmptyState`): Sport icon, “No {sport} games nearby”, numbered steps, **one** Host CTA, invite footer — best-in-app empty.
- **Play · Players empty**: Short copy + strip hint — appropriately lighter than Games.
- **Inbox · per-filter copy**: Games vs Friends messages differ in body text.
- **Primary CTA discipline**: Play empty uses single Host button; Inbox Friends offers “Go to Friends”.
