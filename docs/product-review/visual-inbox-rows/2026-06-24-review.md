## What worked

- **Filter segment** in pill container (`filterSegment`) — grouped chips read as one control.
- **Unread badge on Classes chip** (yellow accent, count 2) — visible without opening tab (tier 4 feature).
- **ScreenHeader** with logo + subtitle sets context (“Game rooms, Rallies, and friends.”).
- **Empty state** copy is specific per filter; primary CTA “Go to Friends” uses shared `Button` (lime + `onPrimary`).
- **Row spec** (code): title `bodyMedium`, subtitle single-line caption, date right-aligned, unread badge on accent — scannable pattern.
