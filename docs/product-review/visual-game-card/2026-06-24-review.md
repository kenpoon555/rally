## What worked

- **Today NEXT UP** (`homeNextUp` preset): Sport icon left, title bold, meta stack (venue · time · distance), spots pill right, chevron — correct glance pattern.
- **Status banner** below card (“Waiting on confirmations · 7/8 ready”) — separates roster state from card body.
- **Hub upcoming card:** Green outline + “Your game” chip distinguishes host session; capacity dot row + avatar stack communicate fill at a distance.
- **Game detail:** Nested location card with **Open in Maps** — clear affordance without leaving app shell.
- **WHO'S GOING** avatar row with +N overflow and ready copy — social proof readable.
- **Code baseline:** `GameListCard` `CARD_MIN_HEIGHT = 88`, preset-driven `showStatusSignal` / `sportIconPreset` — good separation for Today vs Play variants.
