| P | Screen | Issue | Suggested change | Contract impact |

| P1 | Play → Games | First-timer arriving **without invite** sees empty discover — no *"Got a link? Open it"* hint | Add secondary CTA on empty: *"Have an invite? Paste or open link"* | `flow-play-screen` empty state |
