## Named gaps (concrete)

1. **RSVP clarity:** Partiful shows "Going / Can't go" with counts at top. We hide readiness in roster dots and "ready" jargon.
2. **Status-grouped roster:** Coaching `renderRosterGroup()` already ships — pickup doesn't use it.
3. **Next event pull:** Best apps end with calendar hook; we end with attendance admin.
