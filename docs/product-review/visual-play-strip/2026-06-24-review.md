## What worked

- **Strip capacity:** 5 sports + **More** in horizontal scroll — major improvement vs early tier 3 (3 + More).
- **MRU reorder:** Selected sport (e.g. Basketball) jumps to strip front after tap — discoverable without opening More.
- **Segment toggle:** Frosted pill (`SegmentToggle`) reads as one control; three tabs visible for marcus (Games · Players · Classes).
- **Contextual subtitle:** Header subcopy switches per segment (“Find games…”, “Players posting…”, “Browse classes…”).
- **Games empty card:** Sport icon, bold title, two numbered steps, one primary CTA — shippable structure for App Store frame.
- **Players populated row:** `@kunyu` free-agent row under **PLAYERS NEARBY** caps label — scannable.
- **Classes segment:** NEXT CLASS banner + class card with map/time meta — strip + segment chrome stays fixed while content swaps.
