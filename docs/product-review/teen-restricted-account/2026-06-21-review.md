## Primary check — PARTIAL PASS

Teen restrictions hold for a **clean** teen profile (`is_coach=false`, no children): age gate works, Profile hides CPS sections, Add Child is blocked with clear copy.

**Fails contract H2** and creates a policy gap if coach approval is mis-set in DB — teen sees full coach dashboard.

---

## What worked

- **Age gate UX** is clear: 13–17 option explains teen limits before account creation.
- **Profile Settings** (normal teen) has no Family or Coach Tools — matches north-star for default state.
- **Add Child block** is explicit and immediate — not a silent failure.
- **Teen can use core player surfaces** (Today, Play CTAs) after legal onboarding.
