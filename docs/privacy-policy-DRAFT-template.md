## Introduction

Rally (“we”, “us”) operates the Rally mobile application (the “App”). This policy describes how we collect, use, and share information when you use the App.

## Information we collect

- **Account information:** Email address and profile details you provide (e.g. display name, preferences, optional photo).
- **Location information:** With your permission, we collect device location to show nearby activities and related features.
- **Activity and social features:** Information you submit when creating or joining activities, sending friend requests, participating in chat, and leaving reviews as implemented in the App.
- **Device information:** Push notification tokens and basic diagnostic data to operate messaging and improve reliability.
- **Crash and error data:** If enabled, we may use third-party crash reporting (e.g. Sentry) to diagnose failures.

## How we use information

- To provide matching, scheduling, chat, notifications, and safety features described in the App.
- To communicate with you about your account or important service updates.
- To monitor abuse, enforce our terms, and comply with law.

## Sharing

- We use **service providers** (e.g. hosting/auth/database, push notifications, email, analytics/crash reporting) that process data on our instructions.
- We may disclose information if required by law or to protect rights and safety.
- We do not sell your personal information.
