# Delete your Rally account

**App:** Rally (`app.rally.sports` on Google Play · `com.rallyapp` on iOS)  
**Developer:** Kun Yu Poon  
**Last updated:** June 13, 2026

You can request deletion of your Rally account and associated personal data. This page explains how.

## How to request deletion

**Email us from the address on your Rally account:**

1. Send an email to **[kunyupoon495@gmail.com](mailto:kunyupoon495@gmail.com)** with subject **“Delete my Rally account”**.
2. Include the **email address** you used to sign up for Rally.
3. Optional: include your Rally **username** (e.g. `@kunyu`) to help us locate your account.
4. We will confirm your request by reply email within **7 business days** and delete your account after verification.

You can also delete the Rally app from your device at any time; that does **not** automatically delete your server-side account—use the email steps above.

## What we delete

When your deletion request is processed, we delete or anonymize:

| Data | Action |
|------|--------|
| Account (email, auth credentials) | Deleted |
| Profile (username, nickname, photo, preferences) | Deleted |
| Push notification tokens | Deleted |
| Friend connections involving your account | Removed |
| Rally / crew membership rows | Removed |
| Game participation and roster status tied to you | Removed or anonymized where history must remain for other players |
| Chat messages you sent | Removed where technically feasible; remaining participants may still see thread metadata |
| Reviews you wrote or received | Removed or anonymized per retention rules below |

## What we may keep (and why)

| Data | Retention |
|------|-----------|
| **Backup copies** | Up to **30 days** in encrypted backups, then purged |
| **Abuse / safety logs** | Up to **12 months** if needed for fraud, spam, or legal compliance |
| **Aggregated analytics** | Non-identifying statistics (e.g. total games hosted) may be retained |
| **Legal records** | Information we must keep to comply with law |

We do **not** sell your personal information.

## Processors

Account data is stored on **Supabase** (hosting/database). Deletion requests are handled by the Rally developer team; processors delete data per our instructions.
